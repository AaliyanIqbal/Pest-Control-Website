Garden Spraying:

These sprays are used to kill bugs and earthworms that causes much damage to your garden. It helps in preventing any spread of plant diseases such as fungal growth or any spore production.

Some kinds of sprayers:

Compression sprayers:

They work on compression tanks and somehow similar to liquid sprays.

Working:

Hand operated pump builds compression in the tank and allows persistent spraying through nozzle.

Benefits:

It is beneficial in a way that it is easy to carry, and provide accuracy in product mixture.

Liquid sprayers:

It is most commonly use in garden spraying. It consist of fine or coarse spraying depending on the need. Fine spraying needed where more control is required while coarse spray is done to cover large areas.

Benefits:

It is easily available and can be used in large as well as small areas.
